## Attendee Health

**If you feel unwell or have flu-like symptoms, please don't attend the event.** You will still be able to watch the sessions via our livestreams, or the recordings at a later date. If, during the course of the day, you feel unwell, please mask up, inform the help desk and leave the event.

## Hand Hygiene

Hand Sanitiser is provided throughout the Venue. Extra sanitiser is available at the help desk.

## Lunch

Lunch will be provided in individual boxes to reduce risk at meal times.
