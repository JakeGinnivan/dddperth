DDD Perth 2025 will be held at Optus Stadium at 333 Victoria Park Dr, Burswood WA 6100.

## Getting to the stadium

See [Getting to Optus Stadium on a Non-Major Event Day](https://optusstadium.com.au/getting-here/non-major-event-days) for a detailed explaination of all the ways to get to the stadium.

Not there is free parking on non-major event days.

## On arrival

Enter via Gate D and use the lift, stairs or escalator to the registration desks on level 3 (lobby 126 and 135).
